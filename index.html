Venue

Our ceremony and reception will be held at the Syndicated Bar, Theater, Kitchen in Bushwick, Brooklyn (40 Bogart St, Brooklyn, NY 11206).

The Syndicated is a bar, restaurant, and dine-in movie theater in Bushwick.
This is a favorite date night spot, and we can't wait to share it with our friends and family!

  • Subway: L Train — Morgan Ave station — right around the corner!
  • Transportation: Street parking in the neighborhood is limited. We recommend taking public transportation or using rideshares to and from the Syndicated. More information is to come regarding transportation to/from possible hotel blocks.
  • Website: syndicatedbk.com

Travel & Stay

Airports

We recommend flying into LaGuardia or JFK airports, both of which are conveniently located in the area. If you fly into Newark airport in New Jersey, you should be prepared for a longer trip into Brooklyn.

Hotels

We will have more updates on hotels in the future. Williamsburg, Downtown Brooklyn, and Dumbo are all great Brooklyn neighborhoods with many hotel options near our venue.

Navigation

We encourage you to take advantage of New York's robust subway system as the fastest and cheapest way to get around the city!

  • Use the Citymapper app for the best subway and bus directions.
  • Use Google Maps for the best walking directions.
  • You can use tap-to-pay on your phone or card to enter the subway and bus.
  • For rideshare apps, Lyfts tend to be cheaper than Ubers in this area.
